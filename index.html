Our Story

A generous menu of Authentic Italian wood-fired pizza made with high quality produce and ingredients, Pizzas at Froggy’s are hand tossed with their secret art of preparation. With freshly made dough, Froggy has a really special warm and welcoming setting with the best pizzas as well as authentic Italian dishes like home made pasta and risotto made to order to give you the real Italian food experience.
